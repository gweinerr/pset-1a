Galapagos Islands, Ecuador


One of the most desired trip destinations is the Galapagos Islands. The Galapagos Islands is unlike any other island. Charles Darwin, the famous naturalist who introduced the theory of evolution, studied the ecosystem of the Galapagos Islands. When visiting the Galapagos Islands, one may encounter a plethora of unique birds, mammals, and marine creatures including the Galapagos Flamingo, the Galapagos Finches, and the Galapagos Batfish. Tourists will never run out of sights to see due to the wide variety of animals and the large number of islands.


Unique Species discovered on the Galapagos Islands:

  • The Marine Iguana
  • The Magnificent Frigate Bird
  • The Galapagos Penguin
  • The Blue-footed Marine Bird
  • The Galapagos Fur Seal
  • And many more!
