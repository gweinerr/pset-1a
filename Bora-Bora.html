Bora Bora, French Polynesia


Bora Bora is the place to visit if you are looking for an island paradise. It is a tropical island located in French Polynesia, which is made up of 118 islands. There are less than 9,000 permanent residents. The one thing that really makes Bora Bora a paradise is that it is free of any poisonous insects and snakes. The main languages spoken are French and Tahitian. It is one of the most secluded islands on the planet, which makes it one of the most popular destinations for honeymoons.
