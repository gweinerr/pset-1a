2019: Top Five Travel Destinations

By Gabrielle Weiner

The world is filled with a myriad of exotic places to travel. While traveling may seem like a hassle, the memories made and lessons learned will last a lifetime. People who travel have the opportunity to learn new languages and experience new cultures. Moreover, traveling is a great way to relieve stress and enhance creativity. World travellers are able to immerse themselves in another country's culture. Although everyone has their own opinions, I have compiled my own list of the top five most beautiful places to explore in 2019.


Top Five Travel Destinations of 2019:

  1. Galapagos Islands
  2. Santorini
  3. Kauai
  4. Zhangye
  5. Bora Bora
