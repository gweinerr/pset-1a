Zhangye, China


If you are interested in visiting a historic and cultural city with amazing natural and human landscapes, then a fabulous tourist option is Zhangye, China. It was once an outpost connecting Central Asia to the Chinese Empire via the Silk Road. Marco Polo is rumored to have spent a year here in 1274, when he journeyed to China. The Mongol Emperor Kubla Khan is said to have been born in Zhangye. The city has a cold desert climate with very warm summers and cold and very dry winters. Some of the most popular restaurants are Xiao LaJiao, Liang Mao Restaurant and Xianglilai Cake.
